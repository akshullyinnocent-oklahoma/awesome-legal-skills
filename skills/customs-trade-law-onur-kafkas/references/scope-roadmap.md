# Scope Roadmap

## v1.1 (Current)

- HTS tariff classification (GRI 1-6, Additional U.S. Rules)
- CROSS ruling research and analysis
- CIT/CAFC decision analysis
- Duty rate compilation (Column 1 General, Special Programs, Chapter 99 surcharges, AD/CVD issue spotting, MPF, HMF)
- Country of origin analysis (marking, FTA, TAA, substantial transformation)
- Chapter 99 / Section 301 / 232 / 201 current-status screening
- PGA screening and UFLPA / forced labor issue spotting
- Full compliance review (classification + duty + origin + PGA screening + UFLPA + evidence appendix)

---

## v1.2 — Customs Valuation

- Transaction value determination (19 U.S.C. § 1401a)
- Assists (tools, dies, molds, engineering — additions to value)
- Royalties and license fees (dutiable vs. non-dutiable)
- Related-party transactions (circumstances of sale test)
- First-sale valuation (for multi-tiered transactions)
- Deductive and computed value (fallback methods)

## v1.3 — Entry & Post-Entry

- Entry types (formal, informal, consumption, warehouse)
- Entry timelines and liquidation
- Protest procedures (19 U.S.C. § 1514) and deadlines
- Reconciliation entries
- Prior disclosure (19 U.S.C. § 1592(c)(4))

## v1.4 — AD/CVD Deep Dive

- Cash deposit rates vs. final assessment
- Annual administrative reviews
- Scope rulings and circumvention inquiries
- Evasion investigations (EAPA / 19 U.S.C. § 1517)
- New shipper reviews

## v1.5 — Quota & TRQ

- Absolute quotas
- Tariff-rate quotas (in-quota vs. over-quota rates)
- Quota fill monitoring
- Textile quotas and visa requirements

## v1.6 — Foreign Trade Zones

- FTZ admission and manipulation
- Privileged vs. non-privileged foreign status
- Inverted tariff benefit analysis
- Zone-to-zone transfers

## v1.7 — Entry Document Review

- Commercial invoice requirements
- Packing list verification
- Certificate of origin validation
- Entry summary (CBP Form 7501) review
- ISF (Importer Security Filing) compliance
