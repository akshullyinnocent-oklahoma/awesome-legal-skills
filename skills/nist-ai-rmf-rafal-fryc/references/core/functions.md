# AI RMF Core — Function Overview

*From NIST AI 100-1 (AI RMF 1.0, January 2023), Section 5.*

The AI RMF Core comprises four functions — **Govern, Map, Measure, Manage** — that help organizations address AI risks in practice. Functions are broken into Categories and Subcategories. Subcategories are specific outcomes that support the Category.

## GOVERN

Cultivates a culture of risk management. Policies, accountability, training, and oversight cut across the other three functions. See `govern.md`.

## MAP

Establishes context — purpose, stakeholders, capabilities, limitations, impacts. Outcomes inform MEASURE and MANAGE. See `map.md`.

## MEASURE

Analyzes, assesses, benchmarks, and monitors AI risks and related impacts using quantitative, qualitative, and mixed methods. See `measure.md`.

## MANAGE

Allocates risk resources, prioritizes treatment, plans incident response and recovery, and communicates about events. See `manage.md`.
