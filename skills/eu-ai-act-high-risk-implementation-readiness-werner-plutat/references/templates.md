## 5. Simple color logic

### RED
- not started
- materially incomplete
- not defensible

### AMBER
- partial controls
- fragmented evidence
- meaningful work remains

### GREEN
- substantially ready
- evidence exists and is current
- operationally governed

## 6. One-page output structure for quick client delivery

Use this when the user wants speed over depth.

### One-page summary
- system and role
- likely Annex III category
- Art. 6(3) note if relevant
- overall readiness status
- 12-area traffic light list
- top 5 actions
- biggest assessment route risk
- immediate next meeting agenda

---

## Practical note

These templates work best when they are evidence-backed. Do not fill them with abstract assumptions if real documentation can be requested instead.
